Why use Travelwards?

Powerful Dynamic Search

Use our dynamic search platform to find the best instant pricing from over 650 000 hotels worldwide. And we are always adding hotel wholesalers, so you continuously get more hotels, more rooms and better prices.

Customised Vouchers

You can easily upload your own logo to hotel vouchers through the website so your brand is the only one your client sees.

Customer Service

Our booking system is designed for easy quick bookings, but if anything was to go wrong you can contact our customer service agents over phone, email, or chat from 9.00 until 20.00 AEDT, Monday to Friday. We also have a dedicated US toll-free number for hotels to call 24/7.

Free Membership

Travelwards is always free. With multiple accounts and 2 user types, Travelwards is available for every member of your team.

Increased Commissions

We search more wholesalers to get you the best prices, and Travelwards then allows you to pick your own final price, so you get the best deal for your clients and the best margin for you.

Ease of Use

We know there is a huge range of hotels to choose from, so we offer ratings and reviews, hotel photos, facility details and filters. Making it easy to compare hotels and pick the best one for your client, with the best price.
